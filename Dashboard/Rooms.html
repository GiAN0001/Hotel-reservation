OUR HOTEL ROOM TYPES

Single Room: A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds.

The room size or area of Single Rooms are generally between 37 square meters to 45 square meters .

Double Room: A room assigned to two people. May have one or more beds.

The room size or area of Double Rooms are generally between 40 square meters to 45 square meters.

Triple Room: A room that can accommodate three persons and has been fitted with three twin beds, one double bed and one twin bed or two double beds.

The room size or area of Triple Rooms are generally between 45 square meters to 65 square meters.

Quad Room: A room assigned to four people. May have two or more beds.

The room size or area of Quad Rooms are generally between 70 square meters to 85 square meters.

Queen: A room with a queen-sized bed. May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of Queen Rooms are generally between 32 square meters to 50 square meters.

King: A room with a king-sized bed. May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms are generally between 32 square meters to 50 square meters.

Junior Suite: A single room with a bed and sitting area. The sleeping area is in a bedroom separate from the parlour or living room.

The room size or area of Junior Suites are generally between 60 square meters to 80 square meters.
